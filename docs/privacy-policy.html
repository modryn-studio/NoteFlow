Privacy Policy for NoteFlow

Last updated: January 27, 2026

Introduction

NoteFlow ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

NoteFlow collects the following types of information:

  • User-Generated Content: Notes, voice recordings (converted to text), and tags you create within the app
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data about how you interact with the app (e.g., frequency of note access, tag corrections)
  • Device Information: Basic device identifiers for authentication purposes

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the core functionality of NoteFlow
  • Syncing your notes across your devices
  • Improving our app's performance and user experience
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance features (frequency tracking, smart surfacing)

Data Storage and Security

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Backend: We use Supabase for secure backend database services
  • Row-Level Security: Your data is protected by database-level security policies ensuring only you can access your notes
  • Local Storage: Some data (frequency tracking) is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (Hive)

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

We only use third-party services for:

  • Supabase: Backend database and authentication infrastructure
  • Google Speech-to-Text: Voice recording transcription (if enabled by user)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we store about you
  • Delete: Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export: Download your notes and data at any time
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics tracking in app settings

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

NoteFlow is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or via email.
